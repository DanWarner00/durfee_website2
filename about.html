Phil Durfee started this shop after a decade running service for a larger company and getting tired of watching homeowners get up-sold. Today, Durfee Plumbing & Heating is a small crew of licensed techs who actually answer the phone.

Phil personally handles every service issue — if a job goes sideways, you talk to the owner, not a call center in another state. We're small on purpose. It's how we keep flat-rate pricing honest and the one-year warranty actually worth something.

Credentials

Licensed, certified, provable on paper.

Ask any contractor for these before you let them touch your system. Ours are on file with the state and available on request.

MA

Master Plumber license

Massachusetts Board of State Examiners of Plumbers and Gas Fitters. License held by Phil Durfee.

EPA

EPA Section 608

Universal refrigerant handling certification for heat pump and A/C installation, recovery, and service.

MS

Mass Save certified

HEAT Loan-approved. We file the full rebate paperwork for qualifying heat-pump and water-heater work.

INS

General liability and workers' compensation on every job. Certificate of insurance available on request.
