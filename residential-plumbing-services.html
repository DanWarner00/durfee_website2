FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Do you do small jobs?+

Yes — a leaky faucet, a running toilet, a single fixture swap. Most flat-rate jobs start around $185.

Same-day service?+

During business hours, yes, for most plumbing emergencies — leaks, no hot water, drain backups.

Do you take credit cards?+

Yes. Check, card, or ACH.

Do you offer financing?+

For larger projects, yes. Mass Save HEAT Loan financing for heat-pump and water-heater work, and we work with a few third-party lenders for plumbing.

What's the service area?+

Cape Cod, Plymouth, Wareham. See the service area page for the full list.
