FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Does it work in winter?+

Yes — even in cold basements. If the surrounding air drops below 40°F it switches to backup electric resistance.

How big a basement do I need?+

At minimum about 700 cubic feet of ambient air (a typical 10x14 unfinished basement is plenty).

How loud is it?+

About 45–55 dB — roughly a refrigerator. Most basements absorb it well; we don't recommend installing it inside a finished living space.

Will my electric bill go up?+

Down. A HPWH typically cuts water-heating electric use by 60–70% vs. electric resistance.

Who handles the rebate paperwork?+

We do, every time. Pre-qualification on the front end, submission on the back end.
