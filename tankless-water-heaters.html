FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Will I notice the difference?+

You'll notice you don't run out of hot water and your basement got bigger. Most people stop thinking about it after a week.

Do I need a bigger gas line?+

Sometimes — tankless heaters draw more BTU at peak than tanks. We check and upsize the line if needed.

What about electric tankless?+

For point-of-use applications, fine. For whole-home, the amperage draw is huge — usually not worth it on Cape Cod homes.

How often do they need service?+

Annual flush to remove mineral buildup. We offer it as part of our maintenance plan.

How long is the install?+

Full day, sometimes 1.5 days if gas or venting needs modification.
