FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Do I need a permit?+

For new gas lines, yes — always. We pull the permit as part of the flat-rate quote.

Can I run my own gas line?+

Massachusetts requires licensed gas-fitter work on permitted gas lines. We're licensed.

Natural gas or propane?+

Depends on your street. Much of Cape Cod is propane-only. We work with both equally.

How long does a typical run take?+

Most residential runs take half a day plus inspection time.

Do you size for generators?+

Yes — generators are high-BTU loads that often require an upsized line and meter. We do the math.
