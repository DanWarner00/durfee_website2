FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Do I have to remove my existing heating system to qualify?+

For the whole-home rebate (up to $10,000), yes — your heat pump must be the primary heating system. For the partial-home rebate ($1,250/ton), you can keep existing heat as backup.

How long does the rebate take to arrive?+

Typically 4–8 weeks after the inspector signs off. We file the paperwork as soon as the install is commissioned.

What if I rent out my Cape Cod home seasonally?+

You can still qualify as long as the home is your primary residence or a second home that you (not a corporate entity) own. Investment-grade rentals follow a different track — call us.

Do you also handle the HEAT Loan paperwork?+

Yes. We submit the equipment quote and project details directly to your participating lender. You get a 0% APR loan, we get paid on completion.

What if my electrical panel can't handle a heat pump?+

We'll flag it on the assessment. Mass Save has separate incentives for panel upgrades and electrical work; in many cases we can roll the upgrade into the same project.
