FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Should I do a heat pump or central AC?+

For most Cape Cod homes, a heat pump makes more sense — you get cooling and heating from the same equipment, plus Mass Save rebates. Central AC alone is rarely worth it on the Cape now.

What's a hybrid system?+

Heat pump as the primary, a gas furnace or boiler as backup for the coldest hours. Most efficient option for homes that can't go all-electric.

Can you work with my existing ductwork?+

Often yes — we'll evaluate sizing and condition before recommending. Older ducts sometimes need a few modifications, not a full replacement.

Do you install smart thermostats?+

Yes — Nest, Ecobee, Sensi, or manufacturer-native. We wire them properly, configure the network, and walk you through the app.

How do I know what I need?+

You don't need to know yet. We'll do a free consultation and load calc, then recommend.
