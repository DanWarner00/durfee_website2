FAQ

Common questions, honest answers.

Will a heat pump keep up on the coldest Cape Cod nights?+

Cold-climate-rated heat pumps deliver full heat capacity well below 0°F. Cape Cod winter design temperatures are warmer than that, so a properly-sized system will keep up without needing strip heaters.

How much does heat pump installation cost on Cape Cod?+

Most whole-home retrofits land between $18,000 and $32,000 before rebates, depending on home size and zoning. The full $10K Mass Save rebate and 0% HEAT Loan financing can make out-of-pocket far smaller.

Do I have to retire my oil or propane system to get the rebate?+

The maximum $10K Mass Save rebate is tied to whole-home installs that fully replace fossil-fuel heating. Partial installs receive smaller per-ton rebates.

Can you install in an older home without ducts?+

Yes — that's the most common Cape Cod scenario. We run insulated refrigerant lines through exterior chases and mount slim indoor heads on walls.

Who handles the Mass Save paperwork?+

We do — pre-qualification, post-install audit, and rebate submission. You sign two forms; the check comes by mail.

What's the warranty?+

1-year parts and labor from Durfee on top of the manufacturer's 10–12 year parts warranty. If something fails, you call us.
